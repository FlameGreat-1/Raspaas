About Deduction Thresholds
  • The default threshold amount is the maximum amount that can be deducted from an employee's salary in a single pay period.
  • The minimum salary protection percentage ensures that employees retain at least this percentage of their salary after deductions.
  • These settings apply to all employees unless overridden by employee-specific thresholds.
  • Changes to these settings will not affect existing deduction plans that have already been processed.
