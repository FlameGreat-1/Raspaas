Bank Transfer Information
What is a Bank Transfer?

A bank transfer is a method of sending payroll payments to employees' bank accounts. It generates a file that can be uploaded to your banking system for processing.

Bank Transfer Process
  • Create a bank transfer for an approved payroll period
  • Generate the bank file in CSV or XML format
  • Upload the file to your banking system
  • Mark the transfer as sent once uploaded
  • Mark as processed after bank confirmation
Requirements
  • Approved payroll period
  • Employees must have bank account details
  • Payslips must be approved with positive net salary
Important Notice

Ensure all employee bank details are correct before proceeding. Incorrect bank details may result in failed transfers or payments to wrong accounts.
